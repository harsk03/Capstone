A.
Introduction

Remote Photoplethysmography (rPPG) recovers heart rate from subtle periodic color changes in facial skin caused by pulsatile blood flow, captured by a standard webcam — no contact required.

Cardiovascular diseases claim ~17.9 million lives annually. Traditional HR sensors (ECG, pulse oximeters) demand physical contact, limiting comfort and accessibility.

Our system enables real-time, hardware-free heart rate monitoring using only a webcam, making it ideal for telemedicine, home healthcare, and fitness tracking.

E.
Methodology & Algorithms
1) FFT – Eulerian Path
  • Gaussian pyramid temporal amplification
  • Band-pass filter: 0.67–3.33 Hz (40–200 BPM)
  • Dominant frequency → BPM conversion
  • Why? Lightweight; coarse but fast baseline
2) CHROM Algorithm
  • Projects RGB onto chrominance axes Xs, Ys
  • Adaptive ratio α cancels specular reflection
  • Savitzky–Golay pre-smoothing applied
  • Why? Best at suppressing lighting artifacts
3) POS Algorithm
  • Planes orthogonal to skin-tone direction
  • S1 = Gn−Bn, S2 = Gn+Bn−2Rn
  • Adaptive β weighting of planes
  • Why? Robust to skin-tone variation & motion
4) ICA – Blind Source Separation
  • Fixed-point deflation with tanh nonlinearity
  • Whitens 3-channel covariance matrix
  • Activated only when base SNR < 0 dB
  • Why? Excels during high-motion scenarios
5) SNR-Weighted Fusion
  • wm = 10^(SNRm/10) linear power weight
  • Agreement bonus: methods within 8 BPM
  • Gates: FFT/ICA ≥ −1 dB, CHROM/POS ≥ 0 dB
  • Why? Always outperforms any single method
6) Age/Gender Classification
  • GilLevi AgeNet + GenderNet (Caffe DNN)
  • Runs every 30 frames on 227×227 crop
  • 7 age brackets × 2 genders → HR status
  • Why? Clinically personalised HR labeling

G.
Clinical Significance & Future Scope 🚀
A Webcam as Your Cardiologist! ❤️

Our system turns any standard laptop camera into a real-time, non-contact heart rate monitor with clinically relevant accuracy — no wearable, no electrode, no cost. It runs entirely in Python on a basic laptop, making it accessible to millions for home health monitoring and telemedicine.

Future directions: Deep rPPG models (PhysNet, EfficientPhys) · Respiratory rate from same ROI · HRV metrics (SDNN, RMSSD) for stress detection · Edge deployment on Raspberry Pi / Jetson Nano · Evaluation on MAHNOB-HCI & COHFACE benchmarks
